Welcome!

I am an Assistant Professor at Örebro Universitet. I am also joining the Swedish National Institute of Economic Research (NIER) as an Economic Analyst, focusing on data analysis and empirical work. Previously, I held an Assistant Professor position at the University of Liverpool Management School. I am also an affiliated researcher at Lund University School of Economics and Management.

I earned my Ph.D. in Economics from Lund Universitet in 2024, along with an M.Sc. in Economics and an M.Sc. in Finance, both from Lund Universitet.
