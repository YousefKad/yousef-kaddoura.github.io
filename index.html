About

Welcome.

I am an Assistant Professor at Örebro Universitet. I am also joining the Swedish National Institute of Economic Research (NIER) as an Economic Analyst, where I focus on data analysis and empirical work.

Previously, I held an Assistant Professor position at the University of Liverpool Management School. I am an affiliated researcher at Lund University School of Economics and Management.

I earned my Ph.D. in Economics from Lund Universitet in 2024. I also hold an M.Sc. in Economics and an M.Sc. in Finance from Lund Universitet.
