Welcome!

I am an Assistant Professor at Örebro University. I am also joining the Swedish National Institute of Economic Research (NIER) as an Economic Analyst, where I will focus on data analysis and empirical work. Previously, I held an Assistant Professor position at the University of Liverpool Management School. I am also an affiliated researcher at Lund University School of Economics and Management.

I earned my Ph.D. in Economics from Lund University in 2024. Additionally, I hold an M.Sc. in Economics and another M.Sc. in Finance from Lund University.

Research interests: Econometrics, Machine learning, Panel Data, Large factor models, Regularization techniques, Clustering/Grouping, Structural breaks, Finance and Macroeconomics.
